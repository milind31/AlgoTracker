Golden Cross

How it works:

This strategy is one of the most basic and fundamental trading strategies ever. This video does a good job describing it:

We can summarize our strategy with the following rules:

1. Buy when SMA-50 crosses over SMA-200

2. Sell when SMA-200 crosses over SMA-50

What I like about the strategy:

  • Very basic and easy to understand
  • Good for riding massive trends
  • Investments don't happen too often, so we have freedom to make other trades

    • What I dislike about the strategy:

  • Too basic
  • Very possible to miss out on good portions of trends

  • Try this out on steadily growing stocks
